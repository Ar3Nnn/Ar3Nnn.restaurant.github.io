    We are taking all necessary precautions with local city and state officials to keep out restaurant sanitzed and COVID free. Each staffed if checked daily and wears facemask and gloves at all times.
    South Street's menu boasts family crafted neo-soul food recipes including classic favorites like fried chicken, cornbread muffins and shrimp & grits. A full cocktail menu features homemade spiked teas and an array of specialty & old fashion cocktails designed by Bar Lab. Bottle service and an extensive wine and cognac selection are also available. South Street's upper level lounge floods the room with live music from DJs and impromptu performers on the house keys.
